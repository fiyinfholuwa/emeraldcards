  • About Us

Emerald Card Solutions (ECS) is the company behind the Emerald Gift Card and Emerald Corporate Cards brand.
The company was founded with the aim of tapping into the financial mainstream to provide alternative solutions to cash-based transactions. he company offers gift and corporate cards to individuals and corporate organisations.

Emerald Cards Solutions' Credo

  • First At ECS it’s all about relationships. We believe that if we focus on helping our customers achieve their objectives, the company will achieve its own objective at the same time. We strongly advocate; People first, products second, profits third.
  • Products Second Create the best card products and services to help our customers achieve their goal of giving their loved ones the freedom to choose their own gift, and helping our corporate clients streamline cash-based businesses processes with the use of a card.
  • Third If our focus is on people first and products and services second, then profits will inevitably follow.
Our Mission
“To creatively offer a wide range of payment cards that meets the needs of individuals and businesses which will in turn; encourage the use electronic payments, promote financial inclusion and further reduce the risk of carrying cash to the benefit of all Nigerians”.
Our Vision
“To be the leading provider of gift and corporate cards in Nigeria”.
