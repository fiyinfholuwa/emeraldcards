Frequently Asked Questions

What are Gift Cards?

If you want to buy someone a gift but you're having trouble figuring out what to get for them, a gift card can be a great solution. Maybe you don't know them well enough to be sure of what they want. Or maybe the person is someone close you but you simply don't know what they want at that time. That’s why we are here. There's no need to guess. With an Emerald gift card, they can choose whatever they actually want from any merchant or vendor of their choice.

What are Emerald Gift Cards used for?

The Emerald Gift Cards are occasion-specific prepaid payment cards which can be pre-loaded with cash and given to friends, family and loved ones as a gift for them to spend at a store of their choice. The Emerald Gift Cards come in a wide range of design varieties like; Merry Christmas, Happy Married Life, Happy Valentine, Barka De Sallah, Happy Birthday, New Baby, etc. The cards are accepted nationwide as a means of payment for goods and services and at thousands of locations and numerous ATMs around the country. It can also be used to purchase goods online for e-commerce stores within Nigeria like; Jumia, Konga, Quickteller, and any indigenous online store which accepts web payments.

What are Emerald Corporate Cards used for?

The Emerald Corporate Cards are available to organisations to cater to all their various payment needs. The cards come in a range of variety such as; Fuel Cards, Business /Office Expense Cards, Payroll Cards, Staff & Customer Appreciation Cards, etc. and they can be customised to the look and feel of the company requesting the cards with a minimum order volume of 500 cards.

How do I purchase any of the Emerald Cards?

You can purchase the Emerald Gift Cards through our website, by calling our customer care lines; 081-2207-3786, or through the Emerald Cards App on IOS or Android store.

How much are the Emerald Cards?

The Emerald Cards are only N2,000

How much can be loaded on an Emerald card?

The cards be loaded with as low as N,1000 to a maximum of N500,000 at any point in time.

Where can you use the Emerald Cards?

The Emerald Gift Card can be used at ANY store across the nation. It can be used in Grocery Stores, Salons, Restaurants, Spas, Retail shops, and even Online Nigerian Stores. ALL stores accept our cards, provided there is a POS machine to take the payment.

Who can use the Emerald Card?

Everyone! Students, Professionals, Youths, Elderly, etc. Emerald Cards can be used by anyone at all and in any part of the country as long as there is POS machine to accept payment.

How soon will the card be delivered when I buy an Emerald Gift Card?

Your Gift Card will be delivered to you the next day if you won’t be including any name on the card. However, if you want us to personalise the card by adding your name or the final recipient’s name, then your card will be ready for delivery three (3) days from the date of order.

Is there a for delivery charge and how much?

Yes, a delivery charge but it ranges depending on your location so, see please see the options provided at checkout.

Can I buy more than one card at a time?

Yes, you can buy more than one Gift Card but upon activation of the cards, the details of the intended or final beneficiaries will be required.
